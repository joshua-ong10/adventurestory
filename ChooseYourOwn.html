Title: The Beginning of the End
It’s a quiet morning. The kind of morning where everything seems normal, yet a sense of unease lingers in the air. You're Paul—an ordinary guy, living in a quiet suburban neighborhood. Your alarm blares, pulling you from a deep sleep. You stretch, rub your eyes, and reach for your phone. The notifications are endless: breaking news, text messages, and missed calls.

You swipe through the updates in a daze, your mind still foggy from sleep. But something catches your eye—a news headline that makes your stomach twist:
"Unexplained attacks spreading rapidly across the country. Hospitals overwhelmed. Authorities urge citizens to stay indoors."

You shake your head, assuming it's just another sensationalized story. But then you hear it: the distant wail of sirens. Your heart skips a beat. The peaceful morning you expected is far from it. Something is wrong.

You glance out the window. People are moving in a panic—neighbors running, cars speeding past, and others just standing in the streets, staring. You don’t know why, but you sense that something catastrophic is happening.

Your phone buzzes again, this time with a text from your friend, Mark.
"Paul, you need to get out of the house. It's happening. People are losing their minds. Get to me ASAP."

Before you can even process the message, there's a sudden, loud crash from down the street, followed by a high-pitched scream. The kind of scream that shakes you to your core.

You need to act fast. What do you do?
