The Zombie Fight

With adrenaline coursing through your veins, you grab a steak knife from the kitchen and ready yourself. The door bursts open, and a fat, wet zombie stumbles through, heading straight towards you. You don’t have much time to think as it lunges at you with hunger in its eyes.

A long, exhausting fight ensues. You stab, slice, and fight with all your strength. After what feels like forever, you stand victorious, breathing heavily as you observe the dismembered zombie's remains on the ground.

But then, something doesn't feel right. You feel a sharp pain in your shoulder. Looking back, you see a greenish, slimy wound that wasn’t there before. The pain intensifies as your vision blurs. Realization sets in: you’ve been infected.

Before you can react, your body betrays you, and you begin to turn into a zombie.
