Your Fate: Zombie Encounter

Slamming the button to open the garage door, the loud creaks attract what appears to be at least six beaten down, torn-apart figures. With all of them running towards the sound, you freeze in shock. Soon enough, they reach you. Cornered, you have no time to react as they swarm. The last thing you remember is the overwhelming sensation of pain and hunger.
