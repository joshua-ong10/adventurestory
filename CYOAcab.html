Rescue and Safety

Dialing 911, you're beyond surprised that your call actually goes through. After confirming that they can help, you quickly give them your address for their search and rescue team. It's a tense few minutes as you wait, but soon enough, a large truck pulls up outside your house.

The rescue team ushers you into the truck, and they drive you to a large, seemingly secure building. Inside, there are people of all kinds—some injured, some just trying to stay safe. It's a warehouse of sorts, but it feels like a makeshift haven from the chaos outside. You’re safe—for now.

It’s hard to believe it, but you feel a sense of relief wash over you. The worst might be over... for now.
