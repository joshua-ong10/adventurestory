Dialing Your Mom...

You quickly dial your mom's number, your heart racing as you wait for her to pick up. After what feels like an eternity, she surprisingly answers. The call abruptly ends, leaving you frozen with fear and uncertainty. You try to redial, but there’s no answer. What could have happened? Where is she? There’s no time to waste.
