Panicking, You Run Outside...

In a state of panic, you rush outside, ignoring all the dangers around you. Within seconds, the sound of groaning and shuffling fills the air. Zombies from all directions seem to be drawn to your movements, and before you can process what's happening, they rush towards you with terrifying speed.

It's too late to escape. The sheer number of zombies overwhelms you, and as they close in, you feel the sharp pain of their bites. Your vision fades, and your body goes numb as you succumb to the infection.

You turn into a zombie.
